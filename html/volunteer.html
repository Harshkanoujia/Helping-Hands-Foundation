Role: Society Contributer & helper

Responsibilities & Qualification:

  • Minimum qualification of 10th grade or equivalent.
  • Create a comprehensive portfolio showcasing your community service and contributions.
  • Engage actively in community service projects and initiatives.
  • Develop and implement innovative strategies to address societal needs and challenges.
  • Collaborate with local organizations and stakeholders to maximize impact.
  • Basic computer proficiency, including knowledge of Microsoft Office suite.
  • Passion for community service and contributing to societal well-being.
  • Attention to detail and strong organizational skills.

Kindly Note:

Please note that instead of directly applying, we encourage individuals to compile a portfolio showcasing their community service endeavors. This portfolio should highlight the applicant's contributions, initiatives, and impacts within the community. Applicants can upload their portfolio in the form of an Excel sheet, PDF document, or Google Docs link. Upon review and assessment of the portfolio, successful candidates will be awarded a certificate from The Helping Hands Foundation.
